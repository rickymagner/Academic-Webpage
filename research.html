Interests

My research interests lie in the intersection of number theory and geometry. In particular, I like using geometric ideas and techniques to understand number theoretic phenomenon. More specifically, my research focuses on the roles various moduli spaces play in understanding reciprocity laws / the Langlands Program. My thesis (see below) touches on ideas of modern p-adic geometry as it relates to the Geometrization of the Local Langlands Program.
